Who We Are

Designing Systems That Enable Independence

Rera Trust builds systems that help people with disabilities live and work independently. We combine inclusive agriculture with innovative technology to create sustainable livelihoods for physically challenged and visually impaired communities in Zimbabwe.

Combining Physical & Digital Innovation

Physical Innovation

Accessible Broiler Houses

Wheelchair-navigable, automated broiler houses designed for users with limited mobility. Features include automated feeding systems, climate control, and ergonomic workstations.

  • Wheelchair-accessible design
  • Automated feeding & watering
  • Climate control systems

Digital Innovation

Vernacular AI Advisory System

Voice-activated AI assistant in Shona and Ndebele that provides real-time farming guidance. Designed for visually impaired users with speech-to-text and text-to-speech capabilities.

  • Voice-activated in local languages
  • Real-time farming guidance
  • Accessible for visually impaired
